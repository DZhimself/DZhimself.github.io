before contest

I knew the amc contest from my friends who were as motivated in mathmatics as I was. Since I entered my junior school, I was fancinated by the beauty of mathematics and have decided to learn more about it. However, I never took a international contest like AMC. Because of my curiosity and enthusiasm in e, I decided to take the contest. I got past paper from my teacher and asked my classmates and teachers what I should do to prepare for the contest. I knew the maths contest is a long way to go, meaning I have to devote myself a lot to see my improvements. But I’ve never thought I made a wrong decision that I’m going to spend a lot of time to prepare for the extra-cirriculum contest.

what i want to gain?

I chose to take the contest simply because of my interest in mathematics. As a result, my goal in the competition is to simply improve the ability of thinking critically and solving mathmatical problems. Because getting a good mark can show my strong mathmatical ability, I set another goal for myself that I should enter 5% in the contest.

preparation

The process of preparation was hard. I never spent so much time preparing for such an extra-cirriculum contest like this before, which made me quite uncomfortable. But after realizing how much improvement I can get from this contest, I overcame the difficulty in understanding the articles and tried to improve a lot after finishing years and years of past paper. But indeed, there were more difficulties than I expected. I can get anxious and nervous simply because I wasn’t able to solve a problem. I may become blue when I got a low mark in a past paper. As a result, I told myself to stay calm when doing because the reason why I was spending time on mathematics is I enjoy the feeling when I was solving math problems.

during the contest

AMC contest: the AMC contest went quite smoothly. I finished 19 problems during the 120 minutes. Honestly, I didn’t realize the time was going that fast.

AIME contest: I used to think that the contest was too long to stand. However, the 3-hour exam went smoothly,too. I couldn’t spare any other effort but concentrating on solving problems.

what I gained

From my perspective, I gained exactly what I expected when I decided to attend the maths exam- improvment in the ability of solving problem. As I’ve spent so much effort on preparing for the exam, the improvement didn’t surprise me very much. The exam result was OK,too. It is much higher than the first past paper I did which also proves my improvemen in maths.

There are also some other improvements I gained from preparing and attending AMC. The most significant one has to be the increase in patience when sitting in front of the table and solving problems. After the harsh prepare, I gruaduately leant to stay calm when facing difficult problems like that.

more things to do

As a student who is likely to study mathematics in college, finishing AMC12 in grade 10 is definitely not enough. I will study further knowledge in the future and possibly take some contests to prove my math ability. The kingdom of maths is huge and attractive. As a student and as a traveller, I will discover the kingdom with my heart filled with appetite for the enternal beauty.

conclusion

The exam really helped me gained a lot. While receiving a satisfying grade, I impove myself in the ability in mathematics and learn to stay calm. The experience of taking the amc contest makes me more convinced that I should take more challenge so that I can improve myself quicker.

the process of heap sorting：

1.build a heap

2.obtain the element on the top of the heap,which is the largest element

3.forbid the top element and put the last element of the heap on to the top

4.the top element now is the second largest element

5.continuing 3 until the heap is empty

use heap sort to solve TOPK

1.find the first k numbers in a list to make a small heap

2.put away the top the heap(the currently kth smallest number)

3.compare the first number from k to n with the top

4.if smaller:move to the next number

if larger: put it on the top and sift it again

5.continue it

history of physics

the brief history of the development of light in classic physics

** a debate on light– a wave or a particle? **

1.what is light?

Light, something that people observe the most every day, “should be”the most ancient thingever exisited in the universe. But WHAT IS LIGHT? In ancient Greece, scientist used to assume light is something people discharge from their eyes. But this hypothesis has lots of flaws, for example, why can’t people see in the dark if they can discharge light?

When it comes to Rome, Lucretius claims light comes form objects, instead of eyes. In the next thousands of years, scientists figured out the way light reflects, refracts. And as the famous scientist Pierre de Fermat concluded “the path taken between two points by a ray of light is the path that can be traversed in the least time”, people gained a breif view of light.

2.what on earth is light?!!!

​ In Ancient Greece, people used to consider light as a stream of “light particle”. They assume light is a particle becasue of lots of reasons–the way light relflects, they way light travels…..But as people discovered that sound is a wave, they started to suggest that light is a wave, too. The theory that light is a wave can explain a lot of things like : why two light can pass through each other. But they is still one thing that scientists supporting light is a wave can not explain—why light can travel in vacuum (wave travel by the vibration and collision of particles in the media).

3. “the world war”

​ In the following thousand of years,scientists, no mater supporting wave theory or particle theory, tried their best to improve their theory because there are so many things they can not explain, using their own theory. However, things changed as two British scientists appeared on the stage of physics. In 1665, Robert Hook, suggest that the difference in colour of light is because of the different frequency of light wave. Soonly afterwards, Newton found the spectra of lights . He explained the spectra by supporting the theory of particles–white light is a mixture of different types of particles. Both of them were sensitive, ignorant and conceit.They started to attack each other , and kept on attacking till they die. Two years after the death of Hook, Issac Newton released one of the most important books in the history of physics [optics]. He explained nearly any doubt about light using te theory. From then on, Isaac Newton has constructed a complex physics system using his theory in kinematics and light. In the following one thousand years, nobody even attempeted to challenge Newton. It seems that the particle theory got a complete win against the wave theory.

4.the challenger

In 1807, a young British scientist released his famous book called 「A Course of Lectures on Natural Philosophy and the Mechanical Arts 」 In this book, young explained his famous experiment–two-slit interference. The

way the experiment works is shown in the picture below

In this experiment, young gives a strong evidence supporting light is a wave. Only waves can overlap and form the figure like strips. Although the evidence is strong and destructive to the particle theory, nobody that time dared to doubt the theory of Newton. Things changed completely in 1819，a young French architect ,Augustin Fresnel, found an interesting phenomenon, and explained the diffraction of the light. But the theory still leaves something that scientist can not explain—why light can travel in the vacuum? Scientist used to use the word “ether”to explain. But none of them could explain that what is ether.

5.another huge success

In 1887, a young German scientist Heinrich Hertz proved the existence of electromagnetic wave predicted by Maxwell. He finished and completed the theory first suggested by Faraday. By this theory, light is classified as a type of electromagnetic wave, so that it can travel through vacuum easily.

6.mystery never ends

The theory of wave seems to be dominant in the field of history. Everything seems so peaceful and everything seems to be able to be explained by the theory from Maxwell. However, problems are still left.
